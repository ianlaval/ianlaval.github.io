These images are from the northernmost leg of Lydia B's voyage, from Vancouver Island up the Inside Passage to Prince Rupert, out over Dixon Entrance to the Queen Charlotte Islands and back down Vancouver Island's West Coast to Victoria BC. (Text logs begin in Lydialog-1 from Victoria southwards down the Pacific).
